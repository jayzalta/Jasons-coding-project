Lonzo Ball is the oldest of the Ball brothers and right now is a freshman point guard for the UCLA Bruins basketball team.

Lonzo is projected to be the first overall pick in this year's draft and he wants to be like Russell Westbrook because he also went to UCLA.

He is 19 years old and is a 6'6" point guard and almost has a 40-inch vertical leap.

His shooting form may look a little weird but he's been practicing his shooting more than anyone in this year's draft class and he can score from almost anywhere.

This season at UCLA, he is averaging 14.7 PPG, 7.6 APG, and 6.1 RPG.
