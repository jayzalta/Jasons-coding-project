The Ball Brothers.

This is a page about the Ball brothers.

  • Lonzo
  • LaMelo
  • LiAngelo

The Ball brothers are basketball players from Chino Hills, California, who last year, went 35-0 and won the state championship with all three brothers starring.

They shocked the basketball world and showed what the future of basketball may look like with their fast pace of play.

Their childhood was mostly spent practicing basketball and all of that really paid off as their shooting is very good and they even took shots from way beyond the 3-point line.`

Even though Lonzo is already on UCLA, LaMelo and LiAngelo are both commited to UCLA.
